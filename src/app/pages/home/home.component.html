Play and Earn

Mars Siege is a new type of game, partially owned and operated by its players.

Earn MARS tokens by playing and use them to decide the future of the game!

Battle

Build unstoppable troops, vehicles and aircrafts to conquer your enemies!

You can be part of an incredible clan.

Mobilize your army and attack your enemies, prove your strategy skills and make history.

Land

Mars is divided into tokenized plots of land which act as homes and bases of operation for your Army.

Buildings can be upgraded over time using a variety of resources and crafting ingredients that can be farmed when playing the game.

For sure! This is another mechanic within the game. Combine resources to create new items and buildings.
